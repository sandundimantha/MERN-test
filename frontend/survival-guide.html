Essentials

Hit the grounds prepared

Think of PulseWave like an overnight story raid: you’ll need stamina, curiosity, and a little tech to uncover every clue.

Energy & focus

  • Alternate neon drinks with water—hydration booths are every 300m.
  • Use the quiet recharge pods between sets to rest your senses.
  • Micronap lounges light up when a pod is open—follow the cyan glow.

Wayfinding

  • Stages broadcast color-coded pulses on the skyline every hour.
  • Scan city glyphs to unlock map layers in the PulseWave app.
  • Need help? Wave at any teal-clad Story Marshal for guidance.

Etiquette

  • Keep the lore alive—avoid spoilers when exiting an activation.
  • Consent first for photography inside narrative zones.
  • Return borrowed wearable tech before dawn to earn bonus lore points.
