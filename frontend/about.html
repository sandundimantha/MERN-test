Story thread

When a short film became a sandbox world

PulseWave began as a 14-minute neo-noir film that swapped protagonists each time the beat dropped. Fans wanted to step inside, so we built the city, its underground frequencies, and the secret quests hinted at on screen.

Immersive lore

QR hunts, hidden transmissions, and actor-led missions blur fiction and reality across the grounds.

Modular stages

Each stage retells a film chapter—soundtracked live—using volumetric projections and reactive choreography.

Audience agency

Attendees influence the nightly canon by submitting choices in the PulseWave companion app.

Milestones

From underground screening to global residency

2019 • Initial short film

A self-funded short premiered in a warehouse pop-up, gaining cult status for its interactive credits sequence.

2021 • Prototype night

We staged an immersive pilot with 400 guests, testing the narrative scavenger hunt and adaptive lighting rigs.

2023 • Touring chapter

Film festival partnerships unlocked traveling installations and gave artists a platform to remix the soundtrack live.

2025 • PulseWave Festival

The full city-scale takeover launches, folding in film premieres, maker labs, and 24/7 interactive art hubs.
